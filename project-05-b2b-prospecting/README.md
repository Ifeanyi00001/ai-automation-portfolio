# 🎯 Hyper-Personalized B2B Prospecting Engine
**Sector:** Growth / Sales | **Build Time:** ~3.5 hours | **Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐

## One-Line Pitch
> Deep-scrapes target company websites to generate 100% personalized cold emails — achieving 3x–5x higher reply rates.

## ROI
- 1% Generic Email Reply Rate → 8–15% Personalized Reply Rate
- Replaces a Full-time SDR for outbound research

## Stack
\
8n\ · \Apollo.io\ · \Firecrawl\ · \Claude 3.5 Sonnet\ · \Instantly.ai\

## Setup
1. Copy \.env.example\ to \.env\ and fill in your API keys.
2. Import \workflow.json\ into your n8n instance.
3. Configure your Apollo lead list and Instantly sending account.
4. Activate the workflow.
