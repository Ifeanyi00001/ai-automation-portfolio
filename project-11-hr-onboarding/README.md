# 👥 HR Employee Onboarding Pipeline
**Sector:** HR Tech | **Build Time:** ~3 hours | **Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐

## One-Line Pitch
> Automates the full new-hire journey from Typeform submission to signed contract, Notion workspace, and Slack introduction in under 60 seconds.

## ROI
- 95% Reduction in Onboarding Admin Time
- Day-1 Productivity — Employees are Set Up Before They Log In

## Stack
\
8n\ · \Typeform\ · \Claude 3.5 Sonnet\ · \DocuSign\ · \Notion\ · \Slack\ · \Gmail\

## Setup
1. Copy \.env.example\ to \.env\ and fill in your API keys.
2. Import \workflow.json\ into your n8n instance.
3. Connect your Typeform, DocuSign template, and Notion workspace.
4. Activate the workflow.
