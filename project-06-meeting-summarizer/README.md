# 🎙️ Meeting Summariser (Ghost-in-the-C-Suite)
**Sector:** Operations | **Build Time:** ~2.5 hours | **Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐

## One-Line Pitch
> Transforms raw meeting transcripts into structured Notion action items and Slack summaries in under 60 seconds.

## ROI
- Saves 3–5 Hours of Admin Per Manager Per Week
- 100% Task Accountability — No Decision Ever Gets Lost

## Stack
\
8n\ · \Fireflies.ai\ · \Claude 3.5 Sonnet\ · \Notion\ · \Slack\

## Setup
1. Copy \.env.example\ to \.env\ and fill in your API keys.
2. Import \workflow.json\ into your n8n instance.
3. Connect your Fireflies account and Notion workspace.
4. Activate the workflow.
